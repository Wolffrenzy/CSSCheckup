Tanjiro Kamado

Tanjiro Kamado is the main protagonist of the prominent anime/manga series, Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.

Nezuko Kamado

Nezuko Kamado is the deuteragonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. She is a demon and the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Zenitsu Agatsuma is one of the main characters of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, and a travelling companion of Tanjiro Kamado and Nezuko Kamado. He is also a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps.

Inosuke Hashibara

Inosuke Hashibira is one of the main characters of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba and along with Zenitsu Agatsuma, a traveling companion of Tanjiro Kamado and Nezuko Kamado. He is also a Demon Slayer in the Demon Slayer Corps.
